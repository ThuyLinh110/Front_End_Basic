Stroll along some of the most beautiful beaches in The Bahamas and let miles of white-sand and crystal-blue water beckon you to relax in the sun’s rays. Every beach at Atlantis has its own allure and charm. There are three inviting beaches available to guests of Atlantis, Paradise Island. Whether vacationers settle down on Cove Beach, Paradise Lagoon or Atlantis Beach, they’ll find white-sand and a spectacular vista of azure water waiting for them. Just follow a path to the water’s edge.
